Rules of the Game

  1. Press 'Start Game' to test your Baseball knowledge.
  2. You'll be presented with 10 random Baseball Trivia questions from the collection.
  3. You have 20 seconds to submit your answer to each of the multiple choice questions.
  4. After each answer is clicked, you'll get to see a brief summary of the correct answer.
  5. You'll have 10 seconds to review the brief explanation of the correct answer, where you can pause the countdown timer or dismiss it early.
  6. The game will track your correct/incorrect tally and keep it displayed in realtime.
  7. At the end of the game, you can view your final results.
  8. After viewing your results, press 'Start Game' to play again.
